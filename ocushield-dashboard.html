ROAS Decline Diagnosis · Feb → Apr 2026
📉
ROAS declined from ~4.8× (Feb) to ~3.1× (Apr)
Blended ROAS fell 35% in 10 weeks. Spend remained relatively flat while purchase volumes and revenue dropped — indicating audience fatigue or creative wear-out rather than a budget scaling issue.
🌍
US & ROW campaigns dragging blended ROAS down
Non-UK prospecting is spending £3,070/mo at sub-1.5× ROAS. These markets have lower brand awareness and no social proof infrastructure. Disproportionate weight on these campaigns is the single biggest ROAS drag.
🎨
Creative fatigue suspected on top-of-funnel
High frequency on TOF cold audiences likely means users have seen ads multiple times. CTR declining while CPMs hold steady is the classic symptom. Fresh creative sets needed urgently for prospecting campaigns.
🔁
Retargeting pool shrinking
Strong retargeting ROAS (8.4×) with low spend suggests a thinning warm audience. If TOF is not filling the funnel efficiently, retargeting has fewer quality users to convert — compounding the overall ROAS decline.
✅
UK DTC campaigns remain healthy
Core UK prospecting and retargeting campaigns are delivering 4–8× ROAS. The structural performance is there — the issue is budget allocation to underperforming international and broad interest campaigns pulling the average down.
📊
No Google Ads data — blind spot
Google Ads disconnection means the full paid search picture is missing. If branded search or shopping campaigns are underperforming, this would compound the Facebook revenue decline without being visible in this dashboard.
Optimisation Priorities
IMMEDIATE
Pause or dramatically reduce US & ROW prospecting
Reallocate the ~£2,330/mo currently burning at sub-1.5× ROAS in international markets to UK retargeting and proven UK prospecting audiences. This alone could lift blended ROAS by 0.5–0.8×.
IMMEDIATE
Refresh creative on cold prospecting campaigns
Commission 3–4 new creative sets for TOF: UGC-style testimonials, demonstration videos, and before/after eye health messaging. Rotate monthly to prevent frequency fatigue rebuilding.
THIS WEEK
Increase retargeting budget while pool is warm
Retargeting at 8.4× ROAS is your most efficient spend. Increase daily budget by 30–40% while the warm audience exists. Set frequency cap at 4–5 to avoid burnout.
THIS WEEK
Reconnect Google Ads to close the data blind spot
Without Google Ads data, optimisation decisions are incomplete. Reconnect via Supermetrics to understand branded search CPCs, Shopping ROAS, and whether paid search is complementing or cannibalising Facebook conversions.
THIS MONTH
Build UK lookalike audiences from purchaser data
1–2% lookalike from UK purchasers consistently outperforms broad interest targeting. If not already running, create these audiences from your pixel's purchase events and test against current interest stacks.
THIS MONTH
Establish a target ROAS floor per campaign type
Set campaign-level ROAS floors: retargeting ≥6×, warm prospecting ≥3×, cold prospecting ≥2×. Any campaign missing its floor for 7 consecutive days gets paused for creative refresh before reactivation.
